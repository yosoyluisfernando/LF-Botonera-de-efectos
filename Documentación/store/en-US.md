## Description

Keep the right sound ready for the exact moment.

LF Botonera de Efectos is a desktop soundboard shaped by real radio experience. It
organizes and plays effects, music beds, station IDs, voice clips, and music during
radio shows, streams, podcasts, theatre, events, and other live productions.

Build your own workspace with configurable profiles, tabs, and button grids. Each
button keeps its label, color, volume, shortcut, and playback behavior. Play multiple
sounds simultaneously, use loop, overlap, restart, Solo, or Stop All, and trigger
audio with the mouse or global shortcuts while another window is active.

A COMPLETE AUDIO WORKFLOW

• A fixed side panel, global or per profile, keeps essential sounds within reach.
• An auxiliary music player has its own engine, volume, and output, supports .LFPlay
playlists, normal, repeat, and random modes, and lets you mark the next track.
• Independent preview output prepares audio without sending it on air.
• The audio console, currently in testing, provides separate faders and meters for
effects, the side panel, player, preview, and main output.
• Audio devices can be changed while the engine continues running.

EDIT WITHOUT ALTERING YOUR FILES

The integrated editor displays the waveform and adjusts start and end cue points,
gain, and LUFS or peak normalization. Changes are stored as metadata, leaving the
original audio untouched. Configurable RAM preloading reduces delay for frequently
used effects.

ORGANIZE, MOVE, AND REUSE

Create profiles and tabs without a fixed limit, customize colors and grid sizes,
reorder buttons, and drag files from File Explorer. Import and export tabs, profiles,
and playlists compatible with LF Automatizador to move your setup to another computer.

MORE THAN AUDIO BUTTONS

Use individual files, playback folders, time, temperature, and humidity announcements,
multi-selection for button colors, a clock, date, countdown, and stereo meter. The
interface supports light, dark, and automatic themes and is available in Spanish,
English, Brazilian Portuguese, and European Portuguese.

YOUR FILES, UNDER YOUR CONTROL

No account is required, there are no ads, and the current version has no telemetry.
Your audio and configuration stay on your computer. The application does not include
a sound library: you choose the files and are responsible for the required rights.

LF Botonera de Efectos is free software licensed under GPL-3.0-or-later and maintained
by an independent developer.

Supported formats: MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG/Vorbis, OGG/Opus, AAC, M4A, and AIFF.

## What's new in 1.2.1

This release does not add features or performance improvements. It adapts LF Botonera
de Efectos for Microsoft Store distribution: installation and updates through the
Store, improved Windows DPI integration, and fewer unnecessary external permissions.
Audio features remain those introduced in the major 1.2.0 update.
